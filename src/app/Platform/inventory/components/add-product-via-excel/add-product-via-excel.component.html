Upload Products via Excel

info

To upload your products via Excel you need to download the SELLNET Sample file and ensure you populate the mandatory fields. These mandatory fields are the Title, Description, Total Quantity, SKU, Cost Price and Start Price. To download the sample file click on the blue "Download Sample File" button in the top right hand side of the page.
